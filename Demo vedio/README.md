## 📹 Recommended Video Formats

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 1280x720 (HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps
- **Duration**: 1-5 minutes per video
- **Audio**: Optional narration or background music
